What it does
✨

Fixes weak prompts

Spots vague requests like "fix this" or "write SQL" and adds the missing goal, format, and constraints — one click to replace your text.

🧠

Learns your style

Save rules like "always lowercase SQL" or "concise, no fluff." BetterAsk applies them and suggests new ones from your editing patterns.

🔍

Audits responses

After an answer arrives, get a quality score, hallucination-risk flag, format check, and a ready-to-paste follow-up prompt.

📊

Shows your wins

A local dashboard tracks prompts improved, acceptance rate, and estimated tokens and time saved — all on your machine.

Install in 4 steps
1

Download & unzip

Grab betterask-extension.zip above and unzip it. You'll get a betterask folder.

2

Open Chrome extensions

Go to chrome://extensions and turn on Developer mode (top-right toggle).

3

Load unpacked

Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped betterask folder.

4

Open an AI site

Visit ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, or Gemini, type a rough prompt, and click the ✨ fix ask pill.

Private by default

🔒 Your prompts stay yours

  • Works fully offline with a built-in rewrite engine — no account, no API key needed.
  • Everything is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server by default.
  • No analytics, no third-party tracking, ever.
  • Optional API mode sends prompts only to an endpoint you configure, with a key you control.
  • Export or delete all your data anytime from the settings page.

Heads up: AI sites change their layouts often, so prompt detection can occasionally need an update.
Token and time savings are estimates, not guarantees. This is an early build — feedback welcome.
