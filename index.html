What ties them together
  • Local-first. Your data stays on your machine by default. Nothing leaves it unless you explicitly opt in (e.g. by adding your own LLM API key).
  • Read-only where it counts. Tools that touch personal data open it read-only and never write back.
  • Honest about estimates. Anything inferred — tone, relationship signals, token costs — is labeled as an estimate with confidence, never stated as fact.
  • No dark patterns. No tracking, no silent network calls, no auto-sending on your behalf.
